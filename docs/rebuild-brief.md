# Rebuild Brief

## Business goal

Replace the split-brand setup with a single 48 North Concrete site that sells the full business clearly and gives repeat pump customers a painless booking tool.

## Brand direction

- Keep the black, off-white, and high-vis yellow-green palette.
- Use the neon accent as a functional signal color, like jobsite visibility gear.
- Avoid decorative overuse. Accent should call attention to actions, status, queue state, and install behavior.
- Keep the 48 North Concrete banner as the public-facing identity.
- Carry the best parts of the pumping product forward without turning it into a separate brand again.

## Homepage goals

- The first mobile screen must clearly say:
  - what the company does
  - where it works
  - how to book or call
- Services, trust signals, and job-fit details need to appear early.
- The homepage should support booking, not compete with it.

## Booking goals

- The booking page is the product.
- A customer should be able to:
  - open the calendar
  - pick a spot
  - enter rough yardage
  - add location
  - submit
- The request should enter a pending queue for owner approval or denial.
- Returning customers should be able to save booking to their home screen and use it like an app.

## Mobile-first rules

- If desktop and mobile ever conflict, mobile wins.
- Primary actions stay thumb-reachable.
- Key booking information is visible without hunting or scrolling through theater.
- PWA install messaging should live on the booking side, not the homepage.

## Trust signals to add in later phases

- Service territory proof
- Repeat-customer workflow explanation
- Project photos or job clips with a clear purpose
- Testimonials or contractor proof
- Equipment/process confidence markers

## Phase sequence

1. Establish the new homepage and booking UI shell.
2. Replace mock availability and queue submission with real data.
3. Add owner approval workflow and notifications.
4. Tighten PWA install behavior and repeat-customer convenience features.
5. Prepare Netlify cutover only after the rebuild is ready.
